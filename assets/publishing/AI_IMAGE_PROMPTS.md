# 神临山海 · AI生图Prompt（高品质版）

> 用这些prompt在 Gemini / Midjourney / DALL-E / Flux 中生成出版级素材。
> 每张图附带：用途、尺寸、prompt（英文+中文描述）、风格锚点、绝对不能出错的细节。

## 1. 起点封面（600×840 / 3:4.2竖版）

**用途**：起点中文网书籍封面、各电子书平台封面
**必须元素**：少女背影、青绿山水、远处高塔、九色水面、书名

### Prompt (English)

```
A cinematic vertical book cover. A 12-year-old Chinese girl seen from behind, standing at the edge of a cliff overlooking a vast mountain-and-water landscape.

She wears a simple gray-blue short jacket with sleeves too long, covering her fingers. Her long black hair flows in the wind. A tiny ink-black dragon (30cm long, pure black like concentrated ink) is curled around her left wrist, its amber eye glowing faintly.

The landscape before her: layered jade-green mountains painted in the style of Wang Ximeng's "A Thousand Li of Rivers and Mountains" — mineral pigment colors (石青 stone blue, 石绿 stone green), no photorealism, painterly brushstrokes visible. Mist wraps the mountains at mid-height like ancient bandages.

In the far distance, a impossibly tall needle-thin tower pierces through clouds into the sky, its peak glowing with faint gold light — this is the Aintower (爱音塔), 101 floors.

At the bottom of the cliff: a circular pool with NINE distinct color bands spiraling slowly — stone blue, stone green, ochre, rouge, crow blue, moon white, cinnabar, rattan yellow, smoke purple. The colors do not mix. They spiral like nine snakes sharing one nest.

Subtle cyberpunk elements: faint translucent data grid visible in the highest sky like a cosmic dome. Tiny golden particles (灵机 lingji) float throughout the air like fireflies.

Color palette: dominated by 石青 (#3C6482) and 石绿 (#468C6E), with gold accents (#C8A96E). Deep black sky (#0A0A16) at top.

Style: Chinese ink wash painting meets cyberpunk. Dunhuang mural mineral pigment texture. NOT anime. NOT photorealistic. Painterly, atmospheric, cinematic.

Vertical composition 3:4.2 ratio. Leave space at top for title text and bottom for author name.
```

### 风格锚点
- 王希孟《千里江山图》的矿物颜料质感
- 敦煌壁画的飞天色彩
- 雾山五行的水墨山水
- 赛博朋克的数据网格仅作为最淡的叠加层

### 绝对不能错
- 少女是**背影**，不露脸
- 山是**青绿色**不是灰色
- 水面有**九种分明的颜色**不混合
- 高塔在**远处**，极细极高
- 龙极小（30cm），缠在手腕上，不是飞在天上的巨龙

## 2. 横版宣传图（1920×1080 / 16:9）

**用途**：社交媒体分享、pages.dev首页、B站封面、公众号头图

### Prompt (English)

```
Ultra-wide cinematic landscape, 16:9 ratio.

A vast Chinese mountain-and-water panorama at twilight. Multiple layers of jade-green mountains receding into misty distance, painted in mineral pigment style (Wang Ximeng's Thousand Li of Rivers and Mountains palette).

Left third: a small silhouette of a 12-year-old girl standing on a cliff edge, looking toward the distant tower. A tiny ink dragon on her shoulder. Golden lingji particles swirl around her like a gentle vortex.

Center: endless layered mountains with clouds threading between peaks. The clouds glow silver-gray, solid-looking, wrapped around mountain waists.

Right third: the Aintower rises impossibly thin and tall, its peak barely visible above the clouds, crowned with a small golden glow.

Bottom quarter: a luminous river/lake with nine distinct color bands visible in the water — the nine colors of the Nine-Color Abyss (九色渊). The water reflects the mountains above but with different, more vivid colors.

Sky: deep indigo transitioning to amber-gold at horizon. Scattered stars visible in the darkest part. A faint geometric grid pattern (hexagonal, almost invisible) overlays the highest sky — subtle cyberpunk element.

Thousands of tiny golden particles (灵机) float through the entire scene like cosmic fireflies.

Style: Chinese ink wash painting meets soft cyberpunk. Dunhuang mural colors. Atmospheric perspective. Painterly brushstrokes visible. Epic scale. Deeply beautiful and slightly melancholic.

Text space: leave the left 40% relatively simple/dark for text overlay.
```

## 3. 角色卡 · 麦洛（800×1200 / 2:3竖版）

**用途**：角色宣传、社交媒体、起点角色页

### Prompt (English)

```
Character portrait, vertical 2:3 ratio, dark moody background.

A 12-year-old Chinese girl, three-quarter view, looking slightly upward and to the right with wonder and quiet determination in her eyes.

Face: round-ish with baby fat not fully gone, clear bright eyes that seem older than her body, slight natural smile. No makeup. Hair: long straight black hair, slightly messy, a few strands across her face.

She wears a simple gray-blue short jacket (灰蓝色短褂) with sleeves that are too long, covering half her hands. The fabric looks worn but clean.

On her left wrist: a tiny ink-black dragon (维塔/Vita, 30cm long) coiled around her forearm. The dragon is pure black like concentrated ink, with one amber-gold eye visible, glowing softly. Its texture is smooth like polished obsidian.

Around her: golden particles (灵机) floating outward from her body in all directions, like she's the center of a gentle explosion of light. The particles are denser near her and fade as they spread. Some particles have colors: stone-blue, stone-green, cinnabar-red, smoke-purple.

Background: abstract dark (#0A0A16), with faint mountain silhouettes and a hint of nine-colored light at the very bottom edge.

Style: semi-realistic Chinese digital art. NOT anime style. Painterly quality. Warm lighting on face from the golden particles. The overall feeling should be: "a small, ordinary-looking girl who is clearly extraordinary."

Mood: quiet power, curiosity, resilience.
```

### 绝对不能错
- **12岁**，不是16岁不是20岁，保留婴儿肥
- 袖子**太长**盖过手指——这是她的标志
- 龙在**手腕**上，不是肩膀上（30cm极小）
- 表情是**好奇+坚定**，不是冷酷不是卖萌

## 4. 角色卡 · 维塔/小墨（800×1200）

**用途**：灵宠角色宣传

### Prompt (English)

```
Character portrait of a tiny ink dragon against a dark background, vertical 2:3 ratio.

维塔 (Vita), also called 小墨 (Little Ink). A 30-centimeter long dragon made entirely of living ink. Its body is pure black — not metallic, not scaly, but the deep matte black of concentrated Chinese calligraphy ink (墨汁). Its surface occasionally ripples like liquid.

The dragon is coiled in a graceful S-curve, hovering in mid-air. Its body is sleek and serpentine — more Chinese dragon (龙) than Western dragon. No wings. Four tiny claws. Long flowing whiskers. A mane of ink that flows like calligraphy strokes frozen mid-brush.

Its eye: ONE visible eye, large relative to its head, currently AMBER-GOLD (琥珀金色) — this means it is calm. The eye glows softly, the only source of warm light in the image.

Around the dragon: droplets of ink float in the air, some forming into tiny Chinese characters that dissolve before they're readable. Faint golden lingji particles orbit the dragon slowly.

Background: deep black with subtle nine-color aurora at the bottom edge.

Style: Chinese ink wash painting meets digital art. The dragon should look like it was painted with a master calligrapher's brush and then came alive. Beautiful, mysterious, slightly mischievous.

Small text reference note: "Eye color shifts: amber-gold (calm), crimson-red (alert), silver-blue (sad)"
```

## 5. 章节题图模板（1200×400 / 3:1横版）

**用途**：每章题图、Web Reader题图、社交分享

### Prompt — Ch1 入画 (Entering the Painting)

```
Wide cinematic banner, 3:1 ratio.

A girl floating on her back in luminous water that has NINE distinct colors — stone-blue, stone-green, ochre, rouge, crow-blue, moon-white, cinnabar, rattan-yellow, smoke-purple. The colors spiral around her in concentric rings, she is the center of the spiral.

Her long black hair fans out in the water like ink dropped into a clear pool. Her eyes are closed. She is smiling faintly, peacefully.

Above: jade-green mountains with mineral-pigment color, clouds wrapped at mid-height. A vast blue sky.

The water where it touches her skin turns golden — subtle, like bioluminescence. Five-colored water droplets are suspended in mid-air above her, each refracting a tiny rainbow.

Style: Chinese ink wash painting meets magical realism. Dunhuang mineral pigment colors. Dreamy, serene, the first moment of an epic journey.

Leave left 30% slightly darker for text overlay (chapter number and title).
```

### Prompt — Ch7 墨鹿 (Ink Deer)

```
Wide cinematic banner, 3:1 ratio.

PURE CHINESE INK WASH STYLE (水墨画). Monochrome with minimal color.

A luminous white deer stands among ancient snow-covered pine trees on a mountain slope. The entire scene is rendered in traditional Chinese ink wash technique — varying densities of black ink creating depth (墨分五色: 焦浓重淡清).

The deer is the ONLY element with a hint of warm color: its single visible eye glows soft amber. Snow falls in large, slow flakes. The pines are ancient, gnarled, painted with flying-white brush technique (飞白).

Footprints in the snow lead toward the deer. In the far background, a faint silhouette of a girl following the tracks.

Atmosphere: silent, sacred, like entering a Song dynasty landscape painting that's alive.

Leave left 30% for text overlay.
```

### Prompt — Ch10 佛问 (The Buddha's Question)

```
Wide cinematic banner, 3:1 ratio.

A massive cliff face covered in hundreds of carved Buddha statues — inspired by the Mogao Caves of Dunhuang and the Longmen Grottoes. The statues are ancient, weathered, painted in mineral pigment colors that have faded over millennia: faded gold, faded cinnabar red, faded stone blue.

In the center: ONE Buddha statue's eyes are OPEN, glowing with soft golden light. All others have closed eyes. The open eyes look directly at the viewer with an expression that is simultaneously compassionate and terrifying.

A tiny girl (麦洛) stands at the base of the cliff, looking up. She is dwarfed by the scale — the smallest Buddha statue is ten times her height.

Golden lingji particles stream from the open-eyed Buddha toward the girl, forming a path of light in the air.

Colors: dominated by Dunhuang mineral pigment palette — cinnabar red (#C84632), gold (#C8A96E), stone blue (#3C6482), ochre (#B48C3C). Warm-toned overall.

Style: Dunhuang mural aesthetic meets epic cinematic scale. NOT cartoon. Ancient, sacred, awe-inspiring.
```

### Prompt — Ch22 天裂 (The Sky Cracks)

```
Wide cinematic banner, 3:1 ratio.

CATASTROPHIC BEAUTY. The sky above a mountain-and-water landscape is CRACKING OPEN like a dome of glass.

Through the cracks: not sky, not space, but a cold geometric lattice of ice-blue hexagonal patterns — the Harvester's true form. The geometry is alien, precise, beautiful in a terrifying way. It pulses with cold blue light.

Below: the jade-green mountains of the 山海 world are illuminated by this alien light, casting sharp blue shadows. Trees bend away from the crack. Water in rivers and lakes turns mirror-still, reflecting the broken sky.

In the foreground: the tiny silhouette of a girl (麦洛) standing firm while everyone else around her is running. Golden particles (灵机) gather around her, intensifying, pushing back against the blue light.

Color contrast: warm golden 灵机 vs. cold ice-blue Harvester geometry. The battle line between warm and cold runs through the center of the image.

Style: epic sci-fi meets Chinese landscape painting. The scale should feel cosmic — this is a world-ending event painted in the style of a Song dynasty master.
```
